Favorite Desserts

  1. Cheesecake

    Image from Sally's Baking Addiction

    Cheesecake is a creamy dessert made with cream cheese, sugar, and a crust. It can be topped with strawberries, chocolate, caramel, or whipped cream.

  2. Brownies

    Image from Simply Recipes

    Brownies are rich chocolate desserts with soft centers and crispy edges. They are popular because they are easy to bake and taste delicious warm.

  3. Ice Cream Sundaes

    Image from Southern Living

    Ice cream sundaes are frozen desserts served with toppings like chocolate syrup, sprinkles, cherries, whipped cream, and candy pieces.

Dessert Tips

Always chill cheesecake before serving and eat brownies while they are warm.
